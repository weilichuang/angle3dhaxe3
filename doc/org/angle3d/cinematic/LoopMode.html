abstract org.angle3d.cinematic.LoopMode(Int)

Available on all platforms

LoopMode determines how animations repeat, or if they do not repeat.

Instance Fields

var Cycle:LoopMode

The animation will cycle back and forth. When reaching the end, the animation will play backwards from the last frame until it reaches the first frame.

var DontLoop:LoopMode

The animation will not loop. It will play until the last frame, and then freeze at that frame. It is possible to decide to play a new animation when that happens by using a AnimEventListener.

var Loop:LoopMode

The animation will play repeatedly, when it reaches the end the animation will play again from the beginning, and so on.
