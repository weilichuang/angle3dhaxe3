staticprepareNurbsKnots (knots:Vector<Float>, basisFunctionDegree:Int):Void

This method prepares the knots to be used. If the knots represent non-uniform B-splines (first and last knot values are being repeated) it leads to NaN results during calculations. This method adds a small Float to each of such knots to aVoid NaN's.

Parameters:

knots

the knots to be prepared to use

basisFunctionDegree

the degree of basis function
